# Validate demand and channel funnel

Type: task
Status: open
Blocked by: 03, 04

Run customer interviews and a measured 6-8 week funnel experiment using verified sample offers and content. Compare website, VK, Telegram, and WhatsApp roles; test trust/transparency versus convenience messaging; and measure qualified enquiries, quotations, conversions, booking value, service effort, acquisition cost, and loss reasons.

## Done when

- At least 10-15 relevant Russian customer interviews are recorded without exposing sensitive data.
- Equivalent sample offers and trackable calls-to-action have been tested across approved channels.
- A decision identifies the primary discovery, nurture, enquiry, and service channels using observed funnel evidence.
- Positioning and segment assumptions are retained, revised, or rejected based on evidence.
