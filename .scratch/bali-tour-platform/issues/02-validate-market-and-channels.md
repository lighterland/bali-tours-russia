## Resolution

Resolved on 2026-07-11 with the primary-source report at `docs/research/russian-market-and-channels.md`.

The Russian visitor market to Bali is large enough to justify a focused validation experiment, but arrival statistics do not establish product-market fit. The recommended channel roles are: website as the owned source of truth, VK for discovery and public social proof, Telegram for nurture and assisted enquiry, and WhatsApp for service/fallback. The viable positioning remains trust, transparency, flexibility, and clear Bali handoff rather than cheapest or undefined premium. Enquiry should stay manual and multi-channel while the business measures source, qualified enquiry, quotation, conversion, booking value, service effort, and loss reasons. Claims about demand, channel superiority, accessibility, trust, and willingness to pay remain hypotheses until customer interviews and a measured funnel experiment are completed.
