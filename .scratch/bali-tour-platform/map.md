# Russian-market Bali Tour Platform

Status: discovery

## Destination

Validate and define a trustworthy Russian-language Bali tour platform that can present packages, receive enquiries or bookings, and hand confirmed requests into a workable local operation.

## Known inputs

- Source conversation: `docs/transkrip_dan_summary_whatsapp.md`.
- Research references: `openbali.com.ua`, `https://vk.ru/balidiscount`, and a shared Google profile for Bali.Discount. See `docs/source-inventory.md`; none grants content-reuse rights.
- Proposed commercial model: 20-25% commission or markup, depending on package type.
- Proposed operator role: Erland handles customer correspondence and coordinates fulfilment.

## Decisions so far

- Russian is the primary customer-facing language.
- The product is broader than a static landing page because it must support enquiry, booking, and operational handoff.
- Local Markdown is the project tracker during discovery.
- [Clarify ownership and business model](issues/01-clarify-business-model.md) - Resolved the initial segment, trust-led positioning, partner responsibilities, assisted booking, USD-based pricing, net-share negotiation position, cancellation principles, customer-service boundary, and meaning of Russian-market presence.
- [Validate Russian-speaking market and acquisition channels](issues/02-validate-market-and-channels.md) - Established a research-backed channel stack and measurable buying journey while separating market facts from demand and conversion hypotheses.
- [Prototype partner-facing website concept](issues/08-prototype-partner-website.md) - Selected a concierge-first premium direction augmented by structured catalogue and verifiable trust modules, with strict placeholder and content-rights boundaries.
- [Define packages, pricing, and fulfilment](issues/03-define-offer-and-operations.md) - Established a six-family pilot catalogue, provisional assisted booking-to-delivery workflow, and post-demo partner intake; operational verification gates activation rather than development.
- [Prototype the customer and operator journey](issues/05-prototype-booking-journey.md) - Locked one concierge-first production direction with catalogue/trust support and two assisted contact paths: team-initiated follow-up from the form or customer-initiated WhatsApp via `wa.me`.

## Fog

- Brand and actual channel performance remain unvalidated. A pilot package catalogue now supports development, while active prices, suppliers, availability, fulfilment promises, and package-specific media remain pending partner review before activation.
- Legal, payment, refund, privacy, and fulfilment responsibilities are undefined.
- Rights to reuse competitor photos or copy have not been established.
- The intended meaning of "in Moscow" is ambiguous: hosting, domain, payments, business presence, or promotion.
